About This Assessment

PHQ-2 (Patient Health Questionnaire-2)

A brief screening tool for depression. Scores range from 0-6. A score of 3 or higher suggests the need for further evaluation.

GAD-2 (Generalized Anxiety Disorder-2)

A brief screening tool for anxiety. Scores range from 0-6. A score of 3 or higher suggests the need for further evaluation.

Important: This is a screening tool, not a diagnosis. If you're experiencing significant distress or have thoughts of self-harm, please contact a mental health professional or emergency services immediately.
